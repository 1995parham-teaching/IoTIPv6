Larger Address Space

In contrast to IPv4, IPv6 uses 4 times more bits to address a device on the Internet.

According to an estimate, 1564 addresses can be allocated to every square meter of this earth.

Faster Forwarding/Routing

ZeroConf Networking

The information contained in the first part of the header is adequate for a Router to take routing decisions, thus making routing decision as quickly as looking at the mandatory header.

IPv6 supports both stateful and stateless auto configuration mode of its host devices. This way, absence of a DHCP server does not put a halt on inter segment communication.
